About the Convention

The world's largest convention about saws. Newcomers can learn how to use them, and longtime users can share tips and tricks. People of all ages and experience welcome!

  • Located at Merryweather Hotel convention center 4, at 12731 Main Street, Dallas, Texas.
  • $7 entrance fee, paid in person
  • Please remember to adhere to Merryweather rules while at the convention.
